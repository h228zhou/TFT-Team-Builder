Welcome to the TFT Cheat Sheet

This is a web scraping tool created for TFT (Teamfight Tactics) players to build their compositions during a game. This website can help you succeed in your games with our compositions builder, detailed champions information pages, and a unique augment recommending system based on player data around the world.

For beginner's guides, check out Robinsongz's TFT handbook, which includes popular compositions and game strategies for you to succeed in your game of TFT.
